Description

Unique to bea Skin Care, these Vitamin Infused Konjac Sponges are fortified with a balancing and moisture-binding selection of Vitamins, including A, C, D, B1, B2, B12. This is in addition to each Konjac Sponge's vital minerals such as Folic Acid, which increases blood circulation, Zinc, an anti-inflammatory, and Copper, an anti-bacterial element.

The rich polysaccharide content of Konjac forms part of the skin’s moisture-containing building blocks, which makes it a natural option for daily facial cleansing. These sponges, therefore, strengthen the complexion's natural barrier, protecting against inflammation and infection.

How To Use

Dampen one sponge and apply half a pump of your preferred bea Skin Care cleanser to one Konjac Sponge. Massage in a circular and upward motion across the entire face for 2-3 minutes. Rinse the sponge with warm water and repeat. Let the skin dry naturally and finish with the application of your chosen bea Skin Care facial serum.

Ingredients

Each Vitamin Infused Konjac Sponge is immersed in saline, and later infused with the following ingredients:

VITAMIN A, C, D, B6, E, B2, B12, FOLIC ACID, COPPER.

Vitamin A

An essential nutrient that encourages new skin cell production. Stimulates fibroblasts responsible for developing new tissues, making the skin firmer and more elastic. Nourishes the skin, restoring its elasticity and youthful appearance.

Vitamin C

Acts as an antioxidant and is the primary choice for lessening the effects of extrinsic skin ageing, lightening dark areas of discolouration that have arisen from UV damage. In its topical form, it fights free radicals and reverses oxidative damage to skin cells. It also boosts collagen levels, improving skin elasticity, and generates firmer and plumper skin.

Vitamin D

An active form of calcitriol, vitamin D contributes to the growth and repair of skin cells. It also optimises the immune system to destroy free radicals that cause premature skin ageing.

Vitamin B6

Reduces breakouts by protecting the skin against hormonal changes. Reduces the prevalence of dermatitis as well as redness, rashes, and itching. Rejuvenates the skin and imparts it with a natural glow.

Vitamin E

Reduces the appearance of age spots and acne scars. Moisturises the skin, making it softer and more supple. Acts as an antioxidant that protects the skin against pollutants, harmful pathogens, and other toxic elements.

Vitamin B2

An essential vitamin for breakout-prone skin. Regulates the secretion of sebum, hydrates dry skin, and reduces itching and redness. Clears up spots and revitalises the skin by enhancing cell turnover.

Vitamin B12 (Cobalamine)

A water-soluble vitamin that regulates the production of the pigment melanin in the skin, reducing dark spots, melasma, and pigmentation. Assists the body during the metabolism of proteins, which promote the growth of healthy skin cells and repair damaged tissue.
